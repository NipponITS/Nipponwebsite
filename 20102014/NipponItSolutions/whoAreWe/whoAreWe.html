Who are we?



We are New, Youthful, Dynamic and Technology Hungry Company with Database in our DNA. Being a New doesn’t mean we don’t have experience, we started this company with 10 Years of Experience on Database products for worldwide customers.

We give best in class solution for Database Consulting, Database Implementation, Database support and Monitoring, Database upgrade, Database Migrations, Database Performance tuning, Database Training. Let it be Oracle, Microsoft SQL server, MySQL, MongoDB, DB2, Sybase, EXADATA, Oracle Database Appliance or Oracle Enterprise manager 12c. we do it all.

Although Database service is our primary focus, we still believe in taking challenges to Design and Build Applications for our customers. We work with a team of experience resources for Application Designing, Development, Testing and Support. With the new world of mobile applications we also develop customer specific mobile applications.

We believe in giving quality services with cost suitable to our customer’s pocket and on Time. We believe in building our people and our people will build our business as they are our ambassadors. Our employees are our strength and we wish to develop them to reach greater heights by developing and sharpening their skills as per latest market trends.

We provide services onshore as well as offshore. We also cater to staff augmentations for Database Services.
